Accessibility

Last Updated: 15th September 2022

This is the Clarets Hub accessibility statement.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: A, AA, AAA.

Clarets Hub is partially conformant with level AA of WCAG 2.1. This means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard, however we strive to make the site as accessible as possible.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Clarets Hub. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on Clarets Hub by sending us a message via email (claretshub@gmail.com) or our contact us form.

We will try our best to respond to your feedback within one week.
