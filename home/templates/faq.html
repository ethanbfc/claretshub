Frequently Asked Questions

This page features answers frequently asked questions about Clarets Hub.

General

Why can I not access some pages?

Some pages require certain permissions to be accessed. If you attempt to access these pages with the incorrect permissions, then a 'Invalid Request' error will be displayed.

Account

Why do I need to input my first and last name?

For the majority of users, your name will not be used by Clarets Hub and will remain private (so other users will not be able to see it). Your name will only be shown to the public if you are the author of an article.

What should I do if I cannot remember my username or password?

Please have a look at our forgotten username and forgotten password pages.
