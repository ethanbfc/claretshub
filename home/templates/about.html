About Us

Clarets Hub is an independent news site dedicated to the coverage of Burnley Football Club. We aim to cover matches, transfers and any other news that is related to the club. If you're a fan of Burnley and want to stay up to date with the latest news around the club, then this is the website for you.

Clarets Hub was created by a single university student looking for a project to commit to over summer. As a student studying computer science and also a massive fan of Burnley FC, the idea to develop a website related to Burnley was obvious. Therefore this site was developed over the course of a summer, with the use of Django for the backend and simple HTML, CSS and Bootstrap templates for the frontend.

Clarets Hub is currently ran by the sole creator, who is responsible for article creation, community management as well as the continued development of the website. We currently aim to publish two articles per week, however this isn't guaranteed due to other real-life responsibilities. There is the possibility of recruiting additional people to help with content creation/website development; if this interests you, then please check out our openings to see which positions we currently have available.

Commercial

Clarets Hub currently generates income through advertisements provided by Google Adsense. This money is used to help pay for the domain name and the hosting of the website. If you would like more information about our current commerical situation, then please have a look at our advertising page.

We are always open to new sponsorships. If you are interested in sponsoring Clarets Hub (or just advertising on the site), then please send us a message using the contact us and we will discuss it from there.

Feedback

We aim to provide well-written, quality articles which are factually correct. If you find a mistake in our articles, or generally have any other feedback you wish to share, then please write to us using the contact us form.
