About Us

Clarets Hub is an independent news site designed for supporters of Burnley Football Club. We are dedicated to covering every aspect of the club, including matches, transfers and any other news that is related to the clarets.

Clarets Hub was created by a lifelong Burnley supporter who was looking for a project to commit to during the summer of 2022. This led to the development of Clarets Hub, with the website being created over this period (with Django being used for the backend and the frontend templates consisting of HTML, CSS and Bootstrap). Since the creation of the site, he has been focusing on writing content for Clarets Hub whilst also maintaining the website (fixing any bugs found and releasing new features on the website).

We currently aim to publish two articles per week, however it may be difficult to achieve this at times due to other responsibilities (please remember all our writers are volunteers so other commitments may take priority). If this becomes a recurring issue, we may need to recruit additional people to help with content creation or website development. If this interests you, then please check out our openings to see which positions are currently available.

Commercial

Clarets Hub currently generates income through advertisements (some of these advertisements are sourced directly whilst others are provided by Google Adsense). This money is exclusively used to help keep the website running (i.e. pay for the domain name and any hosting costs). If you would like more information about our current commercial situation, then please have a look at our advertising page.

We are always open to new sponsorships. If you are interested in sponsoring Clarets Hub (or just advertising on the site), then please send us a message using the contact us form and we will discuss it from there.

Feedback

All of our articles are rigorously checked to ensure that they are factually correct and of a high quality without any spelling/grammatical errors If you find a mistake in our articles, or generally have any other feedback you wish to share, then please write to us using our contact us form.
